  The thesis: an autonomous agent cannot be trusted to police its own
  spending — so the limit lives BELOW the agent, in a Soroban contract
  that sits in the money path. This script proves it on live testnet.

  Actors
    user     your deployer wallet — owns the funds, signs the mandate
    agent    autonomous spender — the ONLY one allowed to call execute_payment
    merchant the payee — receives funds
